About This Data

It Changes Rapidly

This data changes rapidly and might not reflect some cases still reported.

It Only Includes People Tested

Cases only include people who were tested and confirmed positive. Testing availability vary by state. Some areas may not have data because they haven’t published their data or haven’t done so recently.

It's just a bunch of approximations

Data was mostly gathered using external websites containing information about reported covid cases and estimates of the number of staff available in each hospital to determine the cost of PPE for medical staff and ventilators for patients suffering with sever symptoms of covid-19.

Different sources present different data

There are various sources that are tracking and updating coronavirus data. Therefore, sources may update at different times and may have different ways of gathering data.
